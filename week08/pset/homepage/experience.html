Volunteer Work

Vibra Skate

Community Engagement and Events Coordinator Ago 2021 - Currently

We are a Skate organization that was born with the aim of inserting the healthy practice of this sport, enhancing life changes, revitalizing the space with positive actions of care and welcoming. We emerged from the need to change attitudes and rescue Skate Plaza's primary function, which is sport, culture and citizenship. Passionate about our local skateboarding scene, we seek through our work to support and revitalize our community by improving structures, organizing events and increasing accessibility to skateboarding with free lessons. Through many weeks of hard work and generous donations from the Department of Sports and local businesses, we were able to improve the entire Skate Plaza park and skate park in the Resedás neighborhood in São João da Boa Vista. Currently, with the objective of revitalizing the completed structures, we are directing our efforts towards free classes in the practice of the sport, with the support and authorization of the city hall. We have more than 30 weekly students and equipment at our disposal to donate complete skateboards or parts, as well as safety equipment, for students who cannot afford to buy them. Vibra Skate Plaza was responsible for holding two skate events in São João da Boa Vista, and also contributed to the holding of various activities and events related to cities in the region.
